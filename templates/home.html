Your privacy is our concern!

We provide end-to-end encryption for your business or organisation.
Our work flow:

  1. Create private and public keys. Go to profile tab to create a random (RSA) private and public key. Your private key will not be shown to you because an attacker can take a screenshot of your private key. Its important ot keep your private key private.
  2. Send a message. Add in an AES phrase that will be used to encrypt your message.
  3. Encrypt the AES key with the recipient's public key.
  4. Send your message.
